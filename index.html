FAQ

Questions detailers usually ask before starting.

You don't need to be technical. We'll keep the process simple, tell you what we need, and handle the setup where possible.

What do I need to provide?

We'll ask for your services, service area, logo, contact info, booking links, and any good photos or reviews you want included. If your copy is rough, that's fine - we can clean it up and organize it.

Do I need to know anything technical?

No. If we need access to your domain, hosting, booking tool, or form account, we'll let you know what access is needed. Where possible, we'll handle the setup directly through secure account access, an admin invite, or temporary access.

What if I don't have hosting or a domain?

No problem. We can help you get the right domain and hosting set up. If you want us to handle the technical side after launch, we can also offer a maintenance package that includes hosting.

Can you connect my booking or quote tool?

Yes, where available. If you use tools like Booksy, Square, GlossGenius, Calendly, or Jobber, we can usually add the booking link, quote form, calendar, or embed in the right places on the site.

Can you use my current website, logo, and photos?

Yes. If you already have a site, we can review what's working, what's creating friction, and what should be rebuilt. We can use your existing logo, photos, reviews, and service details when they fit the new site.

How fast is the build?

After the deposit is paid and your onboarding information is complete, we'll contact you with a first draft within 7 days. Launch timing depends on revisions, final approval, and the remaining balance.

What happens after the site goes live?

We'll make sure the core pages, forms, buttons, and links are working at launch. If you want help with updates, hosting, or ongoing maintenance, we can talk through a maintenance package.
